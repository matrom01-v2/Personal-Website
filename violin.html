Violin and Piano

I have been playing violin for nearly 11 years now. I started in the 5th grade and have played in an active orchestra all the way up to my senior year of high school. During my time in high school, I began to pick up the piano, but remained self-taught off YouTube. In my free time, I enjoy practicing both and it does not matter what kind of music. While pop music is fun, I do prefer to play classical.
